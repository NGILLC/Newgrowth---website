About Us

New Growth Investments LLC is a Chicago-based real estate and finance company committed to building generational wealth through smart investments and financial literacy. We empower individuals and communities with real estate strategies that promote sustainable growth and long-term success.

Our Mission

To inspire and grow financial independence through strategic property acquisition, financial education, and real estate partnerships that transform lives and communities.

What We Do

- Real Estate Development & Investment
- Property Management & Consulting
- Financial Literacy Programs
- Strategic Wealth-Building Partnerships

Our Goals

1. Expand our real estate portfolio in underserved urban communities.
2. Launch mentorship and education programs for aspiring investors.
3. Create partnerships with local organizations and churches.
4. Scale operations nationwide by 2026 with measurable impact metrics.
